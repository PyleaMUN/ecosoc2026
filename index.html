Economic and Social Council

Model United Nations Conference

1. Introduction

The United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) committee in a Model United Nations (MUN) setting simulates the work of ECOSOC, which promotes international economic and social cooperation and development.

2. Mandate & Objectives

  • Sustainable Development: Advance the 2030 Agenda and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • Economic Policy Coordination: Discuss global economic trends, trade, and development financing.
  • Social Issues: Address health, education, gender equality, and human rights.
  • Partnerships & Collaboration: Engage with UN agencies, NGOs, and the private sector.

3. Committee Structure

  • Membership: 54 member states elected by the General Assembly.
  • Dais: Chair, Vice Chairs.

4. Typical Session Flow

  1. Roll Call & Agenda Setting
  2. General Debate
  3. Caucusing: Moderated & Unmoderated caucuses.
  4. Drafting Resolutions

5. Draft Resolution Components

  • Preambulatory Clauses: Cite the SDGs, previous ECOSOC resolutions, and relevant UN charters.
  • Operative Clauses: Outline programs, funding mechanisms, and responsible bodies.
